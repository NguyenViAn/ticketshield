TicketShield static image assets

Recommended structure:

- `logos/`: brand logo, monochrome logo, app mark
- `leagues/`: league badges and tournament marks
- `teams/`: team logos and club crests
- `stadiums/`: stadium and venue photos
- `avatars/`: local fallback avatars and profile placeholders
- `illustrations/`: marketing or product illustrations

Usage examples:

- `/assets/logos/ticketshield-logo.svg`
- `/assets/leagues/premier-league.svg`
- `/assets/teams/arsenal.svg`
- `/assets/stadiums/emirates-stadium.jpg`
- `/assets/avatars/default-avatar.png`

Notes:

- Prefer SVG for logos and badges when possible.
- Prefer WebP or optimized JPG for large photos.
- Keep filenames lowercase and kebab-case.
